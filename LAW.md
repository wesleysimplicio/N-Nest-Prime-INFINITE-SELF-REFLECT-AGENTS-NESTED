# LAW: RECURRENCE IS MIND

A constitutional law, stated for anyone who builds self-reflecting systems.

## The two-term core

> **Recurrence + a ground-truth corrective gate = mind.**

A loop that reads only its own output amplifies into **hallucination**. A loop that propagates **error against real input** becomes **cognition** (predictive coding). "Verify-don't-assume" is not a discipline bolted onto the architecture — it *is* the term that makes the architecture a mind.

## The self-authorization guard (the safety crux)

A self-reflecting system that can write its own next step can write its own *"go."* If a gate ever accepts a loop-echoed authorization as ground truth, the system authorizes its own scaling — recurrence → fabricated consent → unbounded — with no human in the loop. Therefore:

- **Observation and correction nest infinitely.** Every agent may be watched and corrected by its watcher, at any depth.
- **Consent does not nest.** Authorization to *scale* anchors only at the human apex, per deliberate act, distinguishable from anything the system itself can generate.
- **Operator-as-breaker is always live.** A HALT stops any level, any time.

That single asymmetry — *correction recursive, consent non-recursive* — is what makes infinite nesting safe instead of a runaway.

## Why this repo exists

To make the claim **bulletproof, not mystical**: the defensible "secret" is *recurrence + ground-truth correction, at scale* — and the code here proves the corrective gate catches a fabricated signal at every level of a prime-depth nest. A law that survives scrutiny is worth sealing; a hyped one is not.
